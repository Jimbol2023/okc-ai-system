# Operational Survivability Readiness Standards

## Purpose

Operational survivability readiness verifies whether future runtime review could preserve constitutional continuity under stress.

## Readiness Standards

A future runtime candidate must be reviewable for:

- shutdown survivability;
- rollback survivability;
- audit survivability;
- provider outage survivability;
- adapter failure survivability;
- entity separation survivability;
- treasury isolation survivability;
- communication traceability;
- human review continuity;
- shared-core non-mutation.

## Non-Execution Boundary

This standard does not activate operations. It defines what survivability evidence must exist before future operational review.

## Rejection Standard

If operational survivability depends on hidden automation, unavailable provider claims, irreversible state, or unclear human authority, readiness fails.
